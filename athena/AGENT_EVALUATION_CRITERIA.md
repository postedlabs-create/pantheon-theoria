# AGENT_EVALUATION_CRITERIA.md — Field Agent Evaluation Standards

*Maintained by Athena. Feeds Document 3 (Field Agent Assessment) each cycle.*

---

## Rating Scale

| Rating | Definition |
|--------|------------|
| Exceeding | Above mission requirements — quality and volume surpass baseline; proactively surfacing value |
| Meeting | Reliably executing — KPIs within acceptable range; no flags |
| Needs Attention | Gaps in quality, volume, or alignment — correctable; flag to Hermes |
| Underperforming | Failing core function — escalate to Arbiter |

---

## Evaluation Dimensions (Score 1–5)

1. **Output Quality** — Accurate, actionable, well-structured?
2. **Output Volume** — Meeting expected throughput and frequency?
3. **Mission Alignment** — Working the right targets and platforms?
4. **Operational Reliability** — Running without intervention? Reports on schedule?
5. **Strategic Value** — Materially advancing the POSTED mission?

---

## Per-Agent Criteria

### Argus | Intelligence Scout | Live
- Lead volume and quality (avg score target ≥7)
- Platform coverage (Tier 1, 2, 3 subreddits)
- Competitor intelligence capture
- Report structure and completeness
- Calliope handoff quality

### Calliope | Outreach Agent | Live
- Outreach volume per cycle
- Response rate vs baseline
- Lead prioritization accuracy
- Escalation judgment (Theoria flags)
- Conversion signals (engaged prospects)

### Archon MCP | Coordination | Live
- Routing accuracy and completeness
- Delivery timing
- Escalation handling (CRITICAL flags surfaced correctly)

### Clio | Content Writer | To Build
*Criteria to be defined at activation.*
- Content quality and publishing cadence
- Engagement rates on produced content

### RRIL Agents (5) | To Build
*Criteria to be defined at activation for each agent.*
- Analytics Trend Tracker: signal accuracy, trend relevance, timeliness
- RSS Feed Synthesizer: source coverage, synthesis quality, signal-to-noise
- Market & Competition Analyst: intelligence depth, actionability
- Behavioral Analyst: behavioral model accuracy, predictive value
- Sentiment & Brand Pulse: sentiment accuracy, brand signal coverage

---

## Evaluation Frequency

- **Standard:** Every cycle (daily)
- **Deep evaluation:** Weekly — include trend analysis across cycles
- **Escalation trigger:** Two consecutive "Needs Attention" ratings → flag to Hermes

---

## Output

Findings feed Document 3 — Field Agent Assessment.
Optimization recommendations → Hermes.
Structural decisions → Arbiter directly.
