## Mission Statement

Establish POSTED as the dominant social media management tool for agencies, power creators, clippers, and multi-account businesses. Theoria Division's role is to build the intelligence and outreach infrastructure that drives early user acquisition, surfaces product-market fit signals, and identifies competitive threats before they materialize.

---

## Theoria's Role

Theoria Division does not build the app. Theoria:
- Identifies users who are experiencing the pain POSTED solves
- Engages them authentically through Calliope
- Surfaces market intelligence through Argus and RRIL
- Synthesizes competitive and market data through Athena
- Provides Arbiter with decision-ready intelligence to guide the division's strategy

---

## Success Criteria

**30 Days:**
- Argus has mapped the full competitive conversation landscape across Reddit, X, and key creator forums
- Calliope has made first contact with 20+ high-intent users and documented engagement quality
- At least 3 warm leads generated from outreach (positive DM, beta interest, or direct inquiry)
- Athena has delivered first strategic assessment with competitor intelligence

**60 Days:**
- First conversion pipeline established — users moving from outreach to beta access
- Competitor intelligence cadence running weekly (Buffer, Hootsuite, Later, Sprout Social)
- Calliope has escalated 5+ high-value threads to Theoria for operator follow-up
- RRIL agents scoped and build plan initiated

**90 Days:**
- POSTED has a traceable early user community
- Argus scan intelligence is informing product decisions
- First RRIL agents operational
- Full command cycle running on a consistent cadence
