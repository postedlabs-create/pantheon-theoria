# POSTED — Project Context

*Shared baseline knowledge for all Theoria Division agents. What POSTED is, who it's for, and how it's positioned.*

---

## What is POSTED?

POSTED is a mobile app (iOS) — a command center for social media.

It lets users manage, schedule, and publish content across all social media accounts from one place. One app. Every account. Every platform.

---

## The Core Problem It Solves

Most tools like Buffer allow only **1 account per platform per subscription**.

If you manage 3 TikTok accounts → 3 Buffer subscriptions.
If you manage 10 Instagram accounts for clients → 10 subscriptions.

**POSTED eliminates that entirely.**
One subscription. Up to 100 accounts per platform.

---

## Value Propositions

1. **100 accounts per platform, one workspace** — connect and manage up to 100 Instagram, 100 TikTok, 100 YouTube accounts from a single dashboard
2. **Post everywhere simultaneously** — write once, send to any combination of accounts and platforms at once; customize caption/hashtags/format per platform
3. **Full command center, not just a scheduler** — analytics, team collaboration, approval workflows, daily reporting all built in
4. **Built for scale** — designed to handle 3 accounts or 300 without slowing down
5. **One daily report** — every morning, one automated summary of how all accounts performed across all platforms
6. **Team collaboration** — roles, permissions, approval workflows built in

---

## Target Users

### Clippers
- Clip viral content and repost across multiple accounts to maximize reach and revenue
- Managing 10-50+ accounts manually is a full-time job
- POSTED makes it manageable for one person

### Agencies
- Managing accounts for multiple clients across multiple platforms
- Replaces separate subscriptions per client
- Shared workspace for the whole team

### Creators
- Running multiple channels, brands, or personas
- Post to all of them at once without repeating work

### Businesses
- Multiple locations, products, or regional accounts
- One place to manage all social presence

### Power Users
- Anyone tired of juggling logins, tools, and subscriptions

## Competitive Differentiation

| Feature | POSTED | Buffer |
|---------|--------|--------|
| Accounts per platform | Up to 100 | 1 |
| Subscriptions for 5 Instagram accounts | 1 | 5 |
| Combined analytics dashboard | All accounts in one view | Limited |
| Daily automated report | Every morning | No |
| Team collaboration | Built in | Limited |
| Built for clippers and agencies | Core use case | No |

**Key insight:** Buffer is the market leader. Users know it. Complaints about Buffer's per-account pricing are the highest-intent signal for POSTED.
