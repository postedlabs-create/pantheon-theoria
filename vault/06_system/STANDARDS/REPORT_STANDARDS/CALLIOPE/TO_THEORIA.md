# CALLIOPE → THEORIA: Flagged Thread Escalation Format

*How Calliope flags high-value conversations to Theoria for Kaizen or Kaiaku to personally respond.*

---

## When to Escalate to Theoria

Calliope escalates when:
- A thread or user represents a VIP opportunity (investor, major agency, high-profile creator)
- A conversation requires founder-level context or authority to close
- A competitor or media inquiry lands in a thread Calliope is monitoring
- The conversation is gaining viral traction and needs immediate operator attention
- Calliope receives a direct question it cannot answer authentically without operator voice

Do NOT escalate routine outreach threads — handle and report in OUTREACH_REPORT.md.

---

## File Naming

`THEORIA_FLAG_YYYY-MM-DD_NNN.md`

---
